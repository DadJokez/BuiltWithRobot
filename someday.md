## 🎙️ iOS Voice Keyboard
Build a custom iOS keyboard extension powered by Google's open source voice AI model running locally on device. Tap the mic in any app and dictate without paying for Whisper or relying on Siri. 

**Why it's someday:** iOS keyboard extensions are sandboxed and complex, local model integration requires Core ML conversion, and getting it to work reliably across every app is non-trivial. Revisit when iOS development fundamentals are solid.
